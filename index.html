The Method

Trade With
The House

Step 01
See The Levels

The dashboard shows real liquidation clusters — the exact price points where the exchange will forcibly close leveraged positions. These are fixed. They don't move with price. Price moves toward them.

Step 02
Watch The Hunt

Bitcoin doesn't move randomly. It hunts liquidity. You can watch price approaching a cluster in real time — the bigger the cluster, the stronger the magnetic pull. This is the edge most traders never see.

Step 03
Enter The Flush

When price flushes through a cluster and reverses — that's your entry. High probability setup. Clear invalidation. Clean exit. This is scalping with the house, not against it.

Reading The Dashboard

Know What
You're Seeing

Every line on the dashboard means something. Each colour represents a different leverage tier — and how often those positions get wiped. Here's what you're looking at.

🟣
Aggregate
Max Pain Lines

The price level that causes maximum pain to the maximum number of traders. Price gets pulled toward these like a magnet — the exchange wants every one of those leveraged positions closed before reversing.

🔵
Boundary
Trading Range

The upper and lower boundary of the current market structure. Price oscillates between these levels until a genuine breakout occurs. Knowing the range stops you chasing moves that are already done.

🔴
100x Leverage
Red Lines

100x leveraged positions. These get liquidated multiple times every single day. High-frequency targets — when price approaches a red cluster, expect a sharp flush through and a fast reversal. These are the bread and butter setups.

🟡
50-100x Leverage
Yellow Lines

50-100x leveraged positions. These get liquidated roughly once every four days, 90% of the time. Reliable, high-probability setups. When price grinds into a yellow cluster and sweeps it, the reversal is usually clean.

🔵
25-50x Leverage
Blue Lines

25-50x leveraged positions. Liquidated roughly once a week. Bigger price movement required to reach these — but when they hit, the flush is usually deeper and the reversal more sustained. Larger setups, bigger reward.

⚡
The Setup
The Edge

Never enter as price approaches a level. Wait for the sweep — price breaks through, wipes the leveraged positions, then reverses hard. That reversal is your entry. Patient. Precise. This is what separates scalpers from gamblers.
